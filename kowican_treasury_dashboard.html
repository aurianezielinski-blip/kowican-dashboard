Strategic Recommendations & Action Plan

1. Immediate EUR Liquidity Management

Address the critical EUR deficit of €1.46M through structured conversion of JPY surplus and securing competitive short-term credit facilities.

2. Interest Rate Hedging Strategy

Convert 60% of variable-rate debt to fixed rates. The 225 basis points spread (€13,000 total range, €6,500 vs base case) justifies implementing caps or collars to limit upside risk.

3. Forward Contract Hedging for FX

JPY: Progressive hedging 50-70% for Jan-Mar 2025 inflows. USD: Hedge March 2025 outflows at 70-100% to eliminate dollar appreciation risk.

4. Natural Hedge Development

Use JPY inflows to settle Japanese supplier invoices directly. Increase USD-denominated revenue to offset March 2025 USD outflows.

5. Intercompany Netting Implementation

Establish centralized treasury across French, German, and Japanese subsidiaries to reduce external conversion volumes and banking fees.

6. Receivables Investigation & Collection

Analyze older receivables causing DSO discrepancy. Implement accelerated collection procedures and review credit policies.

7. Supplier Relationship Management

Address 64% of EUR payables exceeding 90 days through negotiated payment plans and improved cash flow forecasting.

8. Investment Strategy Optimization

Prioritize highly liquid short-term placements. Only invest JPY surplus if returns exceed implicit EUR conversion costs.
